CLR Information

CLR commands are a fun way to interact with the stream. Each of the CLR commands cost 1000 points.
You have to buy the clr command each time you want to play or show something on stream. It's not a one time purchase.

!clr msg [message]
Shows your message after the command call. Displays it on a random spot on the screen.

!clr emote [emote]
Shows the emote you send in. Currently only works with: Twitch global emotes, BTTV channel and global emotes, FFZ channel and global emotes.

!clr sound [sound]
Plays one of the sounds listed below.

Beer
Bottle
Lielielie
Echo
Datboi
Fuckyou
Zelda

!clr gif [gif]
Plays one of the following GIFs listed below.

Pepe
Billy
Kappa
