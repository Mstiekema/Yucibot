CLR Information

CLR commands are a fun way to interact witd the stream. Each of the CLR commands cost 1000 points.
You have to buy the clr command each time you want to play or show sometding on stream. It's not a one time purchase.

!clr msg [message]
Shows your message after the command call. Displays it on a random spot on the screen.

!clr emote [emote]
Shows the emote you send in. Currently only works witd: Twitch global emotes, BTTV channel and global emotes, FFZ channel and global emotes.
