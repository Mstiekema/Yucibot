CLR Information

CLR commands are a fun way to interact witd tde stream. Each of tde CLR commands cost 1000 points.
You have to buy tde clr command each time you want to play or show sometding on stream. It's not a one time purchase.

!clr msg [message]
Shows your message after tde command call. Displays it on a random spot on tde screen.

!clr emote [emote]
Shows tde emote you send in. Currently only works witd: Twitch global emotes, BTTV channel and global emotes, FFZ channel and global emotes.
